Hi friends,

​

I’m Terrian, and I want to share a bit about what God’s been doing in my life lately. This past year has been one of the most transformative seasons of my career and faith walk.

​

In January 2024, I released my debut full-length album, Give It Time. This project was born out of a season of waiting—learning to trust God’s timing even when things didn’t unfold the way I expected. One of the songs closest to my heart from the album is “He Sees You.” I had the opportunity to perform an acoustic version of it for the GRAMMYs’ Positive Vibes Only series, and it was a powerful reminder that God sees us fully and loves us completely.

​

In February 2025, I released an extended version of the album titled Give It More Time, which includes three new songs and collaborations with artists like Joe L Barnes and Anike. The response has been overwhelming—Spotify featured me on the cover of their Top Christian & Gospel playlist, and Amazon Music placed “Hold Me Up” at the top of their Fresh Christian Music playlist.

​

This journey has also led me to incredible stages. I made my national TV debut on Fox & Friends over Easter weekend and had the honor of performing at the Grand Ole Opry. I was also nominated for Female Artist of the Year at the 2025 K-LOVE Fan Awards.

​

Beyond the music, I remain committed to mentoring young girls through AngelStreet Memphis, an organization that empowers girls through music and mentorship. It's a joy to pour into the next generation and watch them discover their God-given potential.

​

As I continue on this journey, I’m reminded that waiting on God is never wasted. He’s always working, even in the silence. Thank you for walking with me, supporting the music, and sharing in this journey of faith.

With love and gratitude,

​

Terrian
