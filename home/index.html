The Story

From a season of waiting.

Terrian’s debut album Give It Time was born out of learning to trust God’s timing. Since then she’s performed for the GRAMMYs’ Positive Vibes Only, made her national TV debut, and taken the stage at the Grand Ole Opry.

Beyond the music, she pours into the next generation through AngelStreet Memphis — empowering young girls through mentorship and song.
